# Harvest Home Guides — Series Plan

> **Brand:** Harvest Home Guides  
> **Tagline:** "Grow What Thrives Where You Live"  
> **Format:** Ebook-first (Kindle + paperback POD)  
> **Target Price:** $9.99 Kindle / $14.99 paperback  
> **Series Size:** 10 books (Phase 1: 3 books, Phase 2: 4 books, Phase 3: 3 books)

---

## Phase 1 — Launch Titles (Q2–Q3 2026)

### Book 1: Texas Vegetable Gardening
- **USDA Zones:** 7a–10a
- **Sub-regions:** North TX/DFW, Central TX/Austin-SA, Gulf Coast/Houston, South TX/Rio Grande Valley, West TX/El Paso
- **Key differentiator:** Only modern, comprehensive Texas-only vegetable guide. Month-by-month + week-by-week by sub-region. Water-smart techniques for drought.
- **Market:** 30M population, massive Home Depot/gardening culture, huge search volume
- **Target pub:** April 2026 (catch spring planting season)

### Book 2: Florida Vegetable Gardening
- **USDA Zones:** 8b–11a
- **Sub-regions:** North FL/Panhandle, Central FL/Orlando-Tampa, South FL/Miami-Keys
- **Key differentiator:** Year-round growing calendar. Subtropical/tropical techniques. Hurricane season prep. Sandy soil solutions.
- **Market:** 22M population, year-round growing = year-round book sales
- **Target pub:** May 2026

### Book 3: Southeast Vegetable Gardening
- **USDA Zones:** 6b–9a
- **States:** GA, NC, SC, AL, TN, VA, MS, AR, KY, WV, LA
- **Key differentiator:** Updated for 2026 zones. Heat/humidity-focused. Direct competitor to Timber Press's 2013 edition.
- **Market:** 60M+ population across 11 states
- **Target pub:** June 2026

## Series-Wide Features

Every book includes:
1. **Updated 2026 USDA Zone Maps** with sub-regional detail
2. **Month-by-month task calendar** (the format readers love)
3. **Week-by-week planting charts** by sub-region within the book's coverage area
4. **50+ vegetable profiles** with region-specific variety recommendations
5. **"First Garden" quick-start chapter** for complete beginners
6. **Container & raised bed adaptations** for every crop
7. **Pest & disease guide** specific to the region
8. **Companion planting charts**
9. **Seed starting & transplant schedules**
10. **Harvest, storage & preservation tips**
11. **QR codes** to companion resources (printable calendars, video tutorials)
12. **Climate resilience section** — adapting to heat, drought, flooding, storms

## Brand Identity
- **Covers:** Clean, modern design with regional photography. Consistent series look.
- **Tone:** Friendly expert neighbor, not academic. Practical over theoretical.
- **Author approach:** Regional expert contributors (or ghostwritten with expert review)
- **Page count:** 200–250 pages (substantial enough for print, manageable for ebook)
